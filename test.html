Component 1: Decision Logic

Rules use case inputs: lead time increase, airfreight rate, cost spike, DC safety buffer, and distributor SLA threshold.

Rule 1 - Shipment Delay Trigger, less than one week
IF lead time increase due to congestion is more than 1 day and up to 7 days, THEN trigger a yellow alert. If buffer stock is sufficient, use buffer stock to cover demand during delay. If buffer stock is insufficient, alert the company to renegotiate with customers and evaluate alternatives within 24 hours.
Rule 2 - Shipment Delay Trigger, more than one week
IF lead time increase due to congestion is more than 7 days, THEN flag affected shipments in red, alert the company to renegotiate with customers and evaluate alternatives within 24 hours.
Rule 3 - Trade-off Table Trigger
IF lead time increase due to congestion is more than 1 day, THEN establish a trade-off table comparing sea, air, and split shipment by cost, lead time, and risk to serve for the evaluation and choice of alternatives.
Rule 4 - DC Safety Buffer Breach Trigger
IF projected DC stock level falls below 1 week of average demand, THEN flag the DC as under-stocked in red and recommend either emergency replenishment from an alternative DC or early release from existing DC stock, based on cost effectiveness.
Rule 5 - Distributor SLA Breach Trigger
IF distributor fulfillment rate falls below 95% in any rolling 4-week window, THEN flag the distributor as red, diagnose root cause, and recommend corrective actions such as expedited inbound shipment, inventory reallocation, or priority DC handling.

Component 4: AI Value Statement

Where AI adds the most value

AI adds the greatest value in predictive disruption detection. The current rule-based DIS reacts once a delay is recorded, while AI can detect early signals before port congestion is confirmed.

Data AI would need

  • Real-time AIS vessel tracking for MK-contracted vessels
  • Historical port congestion records at destination ports
  • Weekly DC stock level and demand data
  • Daily distributor fulfillment logs
  • Freight rate and airfreight capacity data

Business outcome improved

Earlier disruption detection gives MK more time to book airfreight, protect critical Product A orders, reduce emergency logistics premiums, and maintain the 98% customer fulfillment target.

AI outcome: earlier detection -> better mode decision -> lower stockout risk -> protected revenue
